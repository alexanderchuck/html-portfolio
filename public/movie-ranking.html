Movies That Matter

My Top 5 Favorite Movies

5. The Perks of Being a Wallflower

The ultimate coming-of-age drama with a killer soundtrack and emotional impact.

4. Lord of the Rings (Trilogy)

J.R.R. Tolkien, the father of fantasy, has his realms realized by Peter Jackson in three epic films.

3. Annihilation

Sci-fi horror at it's finest and an unforgettable original score form Ben Salisbury.

2. Booksmart

Female-driven comedy, yet relatable to anyone who experienced the ups and downs of high school.

1. Eighth Grade

Directorial debut from Bo Burnham that showcases the woes of fitting in at a young age.
